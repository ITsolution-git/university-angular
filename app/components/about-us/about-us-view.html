About Us

The University Paper is the voice of students.

We've got hundreds of writers across the UK reporting on the stories that matter to them.

From lecturer theatres to the sports fields via student bars, quads, nightclubs and gyms, there's nothing we don't cover.

We've got newspapers going out to 16 cities and more than 50 universities across the UK plus a localised news site providing comprehensive coverage to the UK's 2.5million students.

However, it all started on a much smaller scale at Nottingham Trent University in 2013 by student Chris Moss.

The first edition was only 10,000 copies but was embraced by the student population.

Following its success the paper was expanded to cover all the Nottingham universities and then Bristol’s.

Now we have a rapidly growing team of professional journalists and editors at our London head office, who provide support (and the odd contact or two) to our hundreds of student reporters.

We are dedicated to giving budding journalists the chance to produce stories in a professional environment and to a professional standard.

Plus we’re committed to helping them along in their career in any way we can – be that improving their story writing or giving advice on what steps to take next.

We’ve already got a number of our former writers in newsrooms up and down the country and we’re confident of getting many more.
